About Us...

Our Story

Welcome to Wholesome Kitchen Cafe! We started off opening a small cafe in london, so that we could bring our food to life. We knew we wanted to bring what we were doing to an even wider audience, so shortly after opening the cafe, we set about expanding our cafe locations. We now have 4 cafes across London and hope to expand on this!

Our Founders

I met my now husband, and our ceo, matt, shortly after my book was published and we started talking about taking Wholesome Kitchen further. Matt had worked in finance and business development, and we decided to join forces, me on the creative side and matt on the operational side – to start a family business.

Our Team

Here at Wholesome Kitchen Cafe, our focus is on giving you a premium customer experience whilst delivering great food and drinks. Our warm and genuine hospitality team consist of experienced baristas and thoughtful waiting staff to make sure that your dining experience at Wholesome Kitchen Cafe with friends and family will be a pleasurable one. We look forward to serving you!
